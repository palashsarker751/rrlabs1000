Privacy Protocol.

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

01. Data Collection

We collect information essential for revenue recovery. This includes your Account Data (name, work email, business credentials) and Merchant Data (cart metadata and transaction status) synced via our APIs.

We do not store sensitive payment information; all billing is handled securely via Stripe, Paddle, or Lemon Squeezy.

02. AI Processing

Revenue Recovery Labs leverages Gemini AI to analyze abandoned cart behavior. Your data is used exclusively to generate hyper-personalized recovery messages for your specific brand. We do not sell your data or use it to train global models for other merchants.

03. Data Security

Our architecture follows a Security-by-Design philosophy. All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 standards, and all data in transit is protected via TLS protocols. We conduct regular audits to ensure your business intelligence remains private.

04. Your Rights

As the account owner, you have the right to access, export, or permanently delete your data. Requests can be initiated directly through the Founder Hub or by contacting our compliance team at privacy@rrlabs.online.
